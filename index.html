About i3 Framework

I3 aims to provide an Integrative, interpretable, and interoperable deep learning framework to support tissue imaging based biomedical research, by integrating multi-modal data at multi-scales, including tissue level whole slide images in various stains, e.g., Hematoxylin and Eosin (H&E) and Immunohistochemistry (IHC) biomarker stains, Mass Cytometry Imaging (MCI) at subcellular resolution, genomic data, and patient clinical information, among others. I3 provides full resolution registration of whole slide images, spatial analytics methods for tissue images, integration methods to combine data for joint predictions, spatial attention navigation method to guide MCI imaging, and various interpretability methods for informed decision support.
